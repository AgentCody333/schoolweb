    Parent Information session

    It is a showcase of the next academic year in a nutshell covering the curriculum, class-activities, learning innovations , field trips etc. This helps you to understand your child’s progression in the coming year

    Parent Teacher Meetings (PTM)

    PTM showcase all activities that the children experience at the playschool like free play, interesting teaching aids , engaging takeaways etc. It also features lively puppet shows, APJ Music sessions , rhyme sessions and story –telling by teachers .

    Graduation Day is a big day at Dr APJ Abdul Kalamji School where every child gets the opportunity to wear the graduation certificate . We invite you to be a part of this event as your child graduates from one class to another
